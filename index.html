Muslamic Makers 2nd Birthday

Tuesday 20th March @ 6:30pm

WorldRemit

This year we are blessed to be able to celebrate 2 years of Muslamic Makers. Since our beginnings back in 2016 we have put on 14 events, hosted over 800 people and spotlighted a number of businesses and initiatives in the Muslim community.

Our next event will be celebrating our community and the growth of Muslamic Makers. We would like to use this as an opportunity to thank you for all your support as without you, there would be no community. Join us for an evening of reflections, celebrations and networking with fellow Muslamic Makers.
